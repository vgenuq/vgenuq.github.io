Welcome to VGen UQ


VGen is World Vision's Youth Movement - a community of young people all working together to take action in their local communities against poverty and social injustice. At UQ, our exec team works tirelessly to empower our members to follow this mission. We will equip you with all the resources, skills and training you need to feel confident in your actions and to inspire people to follow your leadership. We can also guarantee that, as a VGenner, you will have some amazing fun times, forge life-long friendships and be welcomed into a national community of like-minded, passionate people all working for the same cause, and advocating for the world's poorest.
