Fairly Educated Conference(18-20th July)

This years’ theme will be "Educate --> Advocate", chosen with the view of addressing the partnerships between the facts of Fair Trade & the stories that accompany them.

Fair Trade as a certification aims to tackle global poverty by ensuring that small scale producers receive a fair price for their products and work in safe, non-discriminatory environments.

The conference runs for three days between 9am – 5:30pm. We have a host of amazing speakers + 11 interactive workshops to choose from!

- Educate themed workshops focused on the World Fair Trade Organisation's Ten Principles of Fair Trade

- Advocate themed workshops designed to focus more on the practical sides of campaigning

We've also got a number of networking opportunities including a Fair Trade Market, live music and a cuppa crawl!
